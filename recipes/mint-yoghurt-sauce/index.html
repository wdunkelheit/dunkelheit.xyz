Mint Yoghurt Sauce

published on 13 Nov 2023

Mint yoghurt sauce is a traditional Greek condiment served with meat, salad and other appropriate dishes.

  • â²ï¸ Prep time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Fresh Mint
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Cumin
  • Orgeano
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Finely chop mint.
  2. Add desired amount of yoghurt to a bowl.
  3. Add olive oil and lemon juice to the yoghurt.
  4. Mix until blended.
  5. Add the mint and spices.
  6. Mix until consistent.

Notes

  1. Add other spices as you prefer.
  2. Pairs well with doner kebab.
  3. You can add slices of cucumber to this to make it more like tzatziki.
