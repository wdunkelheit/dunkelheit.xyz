Library Update

published on 06 Jan 2023

I finally got around to cataloging (some of) my personal library and have made a page dedicated to the books of my library for those interested.

The plan is to use this as a means of keeping track of what books I have, which of them I have read and whether there is any content I have relating to it. As well as keeping a list I can access when out and about in case I need a reminder of which books I own. It is also a useful way to share my library with friends that may wish to borrow something from me.

I haven’t decided on a system of sorting the page yet. I checked Luke Smith’s library page and saw that he uses the Library of Congress system. Which doesn’t seem appropriate for my uses. For now I have opted for categorising books based on which genre I believe is most appropriate.

There is also the question of whether to include the likes of manga or mass market paperback fiction titles as these aren’t as meaningful as other texts although most of the ones I find myself owning are of a higher quality than one would find on the best sellers list or the advertising shelves at a chain book shop.

One reason not to include manga is simply that I have no idea how to do include them elegantly. Do I list them by author or illustrator? Many of them have multiple. For now I have settled on including them by name only until I can determine a better way to include them that is more consistent with the rest of the catalog.

If you would like to check out the library, it can be found here.
