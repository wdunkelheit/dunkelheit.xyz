Who Cares About Twitter?

published on 26 Apr 2022

Apparently it’s a big deal that Elon Musk bought Twitter. Everyone seems to be competing for the worst take regarding it. Which means it must be the Current Thingâ„¢ï¸.

The true based and red pilled position is as always “who cares?”

Twitter sucks, it always sucked, it will continue to suck.

Elon Musk wants to “verify all humans”, this effectively means KYCAML for Twitter. Apparently his reasoning for buying Twitter is to secure free speech online or something. A laughable statement when combined with his proposed human verification efforts.

If you want a more free (as in freedom) alternative to Twitter then maybe look into Fediverse services such as Mastodon and Pleroma. Do your research before you join an instance of course. Many of them are more censorious than Twitter.

If you want a truly free instance then run your own, create your own rules, block instances that you don’t like, etc. You can learn how to host your own Pleroma instance with their official documentation or at LandChad.
